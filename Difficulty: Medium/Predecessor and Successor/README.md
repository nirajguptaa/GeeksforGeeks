<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/predecessor-and-successor/1">Predecessor and Successor</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">You are given the <strong>root </strong>of a BST&nbsp;and an integer <strong>key</strong>. You need to find the inorder <strong>predecessor</strong> and </span><strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif; font-size: 18.6667px;">successor </strong><span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif; font-size: 14pt;">of the given key. <p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Note:</strong> In an inorder traversal the number just <strong>smaller</strong> than the target is the predecessor and the number just <strong>greater</strong> than the target is the successor.&nbsp;</span></p>
